About us

About RateKit

Free, fast, and honest business calculators. No fluff, no upsells, no data harvesting.

Our mission: Give every founder, freelancer, and entrepreneur instant access to professional-grade financial calculators — completely free, no signup required.

Why RateKit Exists

Too many financial tools are either buried behind paywalls, require account creation just to see a number, or are cluttered with ads and dark patterns. We built RateKit because we believe the basic math of running a business should be accessible to everyone.

Every calculator on RateKit works instantly in your browser. No data is sent to our servers. No account needed. No credit card. Just the numbers you need.

What We Build

RateKit focuses on practical tools for two main audiences:

  • Freelancers & Consultants — Freelance rate calculator, Stripe/PayPal fee calculator, Self-employment tax estimator, VAT/GST calculator
  • Founders & Startups — SAFE equity calculator, Burn rate & runway, LTV:CAC ratio, Break-even analysis, ROI calculator, Business loan calculator

Our Principles

  • ✅ Always free for core features
  • ✅ No account required to use calculators
  • ✅ Your data stays in your browser
  • ✅ No dark patterns or misleading results
  • ✅ Formulas based on publicly accepted financial standards

Coming Soon

We are working on optional user accounts that will let you save and revisit your calculations. This will always be free for core features. Premium features (branded PDF exports, unlimited saved calculations) may be available for a small fee in the future.
